## 3. Top Opportunities

### #1 — Lockpick page: fix "lockpick trainer" CTR gap
**Query:** `lockpick trainer` | **Pos:** 4.4 | **Impr:** 157 | **CTR:** 5.7% (9 clicks)

This is the clearest miss. Position 4.4 should yield ~12–15% CTR; instead it's 5.7%. The current title is `Lockpick Minigame – NoPixel 4.0 & FiveM Practice Trainer` — "trainer" appears at the very end and users scanning quickly don't register it. The fix is to lead with "FiveM Lockpick Trainer" so the query term appears at the start of the SERP snippet.

**Expected lift:** ~+9–14 additional clicks/28d from this query alone (CTR from 5.7% → 12–15%).  
**File:** `app/puzzles/lockpick/page.tsx` → `metadata.title` and `metadata.description`
